Bootstrapskin Maps

Add Google Maps to your wiki pages (works with both Bootstrapskin and Vector) with nothing more than a div class, great for showing your business address, meeting locations, or anywhere related to your wiki content. Simple to install, configue and use.

Installation

Download and unpack into your extensions folder and add the following to the bottom of LocalSettings.php

require_once "$IP/extensions/BootStrapSkinMaps/map.php";

Add the following to the article where you want to display the map.
