Bootstrapskin Extensions

As of 7th October 2014 we re-released several of our archived extensions, all have been rewritten and updated for ease of installation and use , we've also included some brand new extensions which offer a range of solutions for people that use Bootstrapskin (and for those that don't).

Note that while many of these extensions also work with Vector and Foreground they may require fine tuning of the styling or the use of additional (included) style sheets.

  • VR = tick or cross denotes compatibility with Vector skin
  • FG = tick or cross denotes compatibility with Foregound skin
