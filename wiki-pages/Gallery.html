    Installation

    Download and unpack into your extensions folder and add the following to the bottom of LocalSettings.php

    require_once "$IP/extensions/BootStrapSkinGallery/gallery.php";

    Configuration

    Open to edit extensions/BootStrapSkinGallery/modules/ext.output.js . If you have modals installed at your wiki change line 4 to;

    useBootstrapModal: true,

    Modify lines 10 and 11 and replace with your own Flickr API key and photoset id.
