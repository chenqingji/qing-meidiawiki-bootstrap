Bootstrap Jumbotron is normally used at the top of the page as an marketing section where you can add bootstraps amazing typography. We've added several variations to give you of flavour of what's available.

In the current version of BootStrapSkin only the shaded colours are available for Jumbotron; so replace "jumbotron-lighter" with any of the following;

Also take note of the text classes "lead" and "small".
