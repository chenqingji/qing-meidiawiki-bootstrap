Usage

Nav Tabs is available from version 1.0.42 of BootStrapSkin.

To use Nav Tabs add the following to Mediawiki:Common.js

$('#myTab a').click(function (e) {
  e.preventDefault()
  $(this).tab('show')
})

Note: Each time you include Tabs to an article it will need to be called though common.js - But changing the two instances of #myTab in the article content and when calling the js, just change to suit the article content ([[MediaWiki:Common.js|see here for how we do it!]]).

Refresh the browser cache - Ctrl + Shift + r and then add the following to your article and modify as required.
