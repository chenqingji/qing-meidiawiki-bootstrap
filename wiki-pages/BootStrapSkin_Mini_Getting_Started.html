Getting Started with Mini

http://www.mediawikibootstrapskin.co.uk/images/BootStrapSkin-Mini.jpg

Though BootStrapSkin Mini was designed for all of our skins it isn't as complex as the full version of BootStrapSkin, there aren't as many JavaScripts and the bootstrap styling is less substantial. What this means is that if you have used any of the more advanced (non-standard) classes in your main skin, these will not be replicated in the BootStrapSkin Mini skin.

You can easily solve this by uploading your prefered BootStrapSkin color stylesheet and replacing bootstrap.css with bootstrap-blue.css in bootstrapmini.php, though you should be made aware that this will have a significant impact on the mobile site loading speed and how Mini looks.

My advice is to find the classes you use in bootstrap-[colour].css and then copy and paste them into the bottom of Mini's bootstrap.css.
