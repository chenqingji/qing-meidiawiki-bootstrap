Installation

Download and unpack into your extensions folder and add the following to the bottom of LocalSettings.php

require_once "$IP/extensions/BootStrapSkinSlideshow/BootStrapSkinSlideshow.php";

Configuration

Open to edit extensions/BootStrapSkinSlideshow/modules/ext.output.js to modify the slideshow height and width.

Open to edit extensions/BootStrapSkinSlideshow/modules/ext.slideshow.js to modify the JavaScript, i.e. animation type, duration etc.

Open to edit extensions/BootStrapSkinSlideshow/modules/ext.slideshow.css to have a play and fine tune the styling.
