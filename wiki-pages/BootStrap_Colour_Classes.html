Each BootStrapSkin stylesheet has 23 colours to choose from, 4 Bootstrap and 14 custom colours, and a further 5 shades of the stylesheet being used.

The examples below use the bootstrap-blue.css stylesheet, but you can also choose from 10 other colour styles.

'''Shaded colour classes''';

'''BootStrap colour classes''';

info

'''Custom colour classes''';

In the code below "neutral" is the button colour, simply replace "neutral" with one of the colour classes shown in the example sections.

 neutral

Modifying panels, wells, alerts and any other feature that can be colour styled, the format is (replace with your colour class choice);

Not much can be said with regard to tips for colour classes.

You may however consider creating your own sheet with custom colour classes, which contrary to popular belief is a lot simpler than it may seem. Once you've mapped the main colour and its shades, simply use the "search and replace" feature of Notepad ++ to add your new colours.
