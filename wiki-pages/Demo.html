About Bootstrapskin

BootStrapSkin is a multi-purpose (Mobile Friendly) skin for Mediawiki, that can be used as is or as a skinning template to create dynamic websites for Mediawiki - see our commercial skins using the skins menu above.

There's absolutely no magic involved , or any requirement to add unsecure html, simply follow our guides to create a feature rich, exciting wiki that people will love to visit and participate in.

Latest Version

Version 1.0.43 (released in August 21st 2014) includes eleven Bootstrap styled colours, numerous optional addons, over 30 jQuery plugins and custom extensions, including our popular contact page extension.

Version 1.0.43 is completely stable with no reported issues since May 2014. There are updates almost daily, with new styles and jQuery effects added on a weekly page. Find plugins at our Bootstrapskin members wiki.
