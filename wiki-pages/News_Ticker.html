Bootstrapskin News Ticker

  • [[#|A powerful, flexible and animated news ticker extension for Mediawiki]]
  • [[#|Provides high flexibility thanks to numerous callbacks and methods.]]
  • [[#|Fully customisable and effective for promoting latest news]]
  • [[#|Easy to install and customise, use different news items for every wiki page]]

Installation

Download and unpack into your extensions folder and add the following to the bottom of LocalSettings.php

require_once "$IP/extensions/BootStrapSkinNews/news.php";

Article Input

Add the following to the article where you want to display the news ticker. replace # with the wiki page title.

    

      
    
        
  • [[#|A powerful, flexible and animated vertical news ticker plugin]]
    • 
        
  • [[#|Provides hight flexibility thanks to numerous callbacks & methods.]]
    • 
        
  • [[#|Fully customizable to every kind of vertical scrolling need.]]
    • 
        
  • [[#|Easy to install and customise, use different news items for wiki pages]]
    •
