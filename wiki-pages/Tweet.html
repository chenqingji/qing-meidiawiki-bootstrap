Bootstrapskin Tweet

Bootstrapskin Tweets
| [https://twitter.com/Bootstrapskin Connect with Twitter]

Installation

Download and unpack into your extensions folder and add the following to the bottom of LocalSettings.php

require_once "$IP/extensions/BootStrapSkinTweet/tweet.php";

Configuration

Open to edit /extensions/BootStrapSkinTweet/modules/ext.output.js and add your Twitter username.

Input

Add the following into your prefered article location, replacing # with the url to your Twitter page.

    

      

        
Bootstrapskin Tweets

      

      

      

        
| [# Connect with Twitter]

Vector Use

Also works with Vector, though you may want to consider using vector.css, which will allow you to align your feed within the article.

In which case open to edit /extensions/BootStrapSkinTweet/tweet.php and remove the comments // from line 19 and then use the classes in modules/ext.vector.css. You may need to fine tune the styling.
