iPhone Addon Skin - Bootstrapskin Mini

http://cnet3.cbsistatic.com/hub/i/r/2012/09/17/314619ad-8bb7-11e3-a24e-d4ae52e62bcc/thumbnail/770x433/5e6f2e060dba9e86fe53e67e3f31d36d/06_archimedes_35438535.jpg

Bootstrapskin Mini is a brand new skin for Mediawiki, it can be used in combination with Bootstrapskin or any of our NG skins. Mini can detect how a visitor is viewing a BSS website and once a mobile phone is detected, Mini is rendered in place of the default skin.

BootStrapSkin Mini is a read only skin! There are no system links, the code is smaller (one quarter the size of BootStrapSkin), with less JavaScripts and minified stylesheets. Mini has been designed with speed in mind, it primary purpose is to provide a convenient method or reading wiki content on a mobile phone.
