Other Consideration

BootStrapSkin Mini is a read only skin! There are no system links, the code is smaller (one quarter the size of BootStrapSkin), with a lot less JavaScripts and minified stylesheets. Mini has been designed with speed in mind, it primary purpose is to provide a convenient method or reading your wiki content on a mobile phone.

BootStrapSkin Mini still uses Bootstrap, though there are less style classes than BootStrapSkin, but several more than the standard Bootstrap. We've also included lightbox and Nav Tabs along with more stylish buttons, alerts and panels, Font Awesome is also included.

If you use our Contact Form extension this works with Mini too.

Customisation

There shouldn't be any need to customise the skin other than to change the links, which you can modify easily (lines 160 - 163 of Bootstrapskinmini.skin.php).
